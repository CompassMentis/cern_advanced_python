Type: Margherita Pizza

Ingredients:
Pizza dough
Tomato sauce
Mozzarella cheese
Fresh basil leaves
Olive oil
Salt and pepper

Recipe:
Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
Roll out the pizza dough into your desired shape and thickness.
Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough.
Sprinkle mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
Scatter fresh basil leaves on top of the cheese.
Drizzle olive oil over the pizza and season with salt and pepper to taste.
Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes

Type: Pepperoni Pizza:

Ingredients:
Pizza dough
Tomato sauce
Mozzarella cheese
Pepperoni slices

Recipe:
Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
Roll out the pizza dough into your desired shape and thickness.
Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough.
Sprinkle mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
Arrange pepperoni slices on top of the cheese.

Type: BBQ Chicken Pizza:
Ingredients:
Pizza dough
BBQ sauce
Cooked chicken breast, shredded
Red onion, thinly sliced
Mozzarella cheese

Recipe:
Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
Roll out the pizza dough into your desired shape and thickness.
Spread a layer of BBQ sauce over the dough.
Scatter shredded cooked chicken and thinly sliced red onion over the sauce.
Sprinkle mozzarella cheese evenly over the toppings.
Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes
